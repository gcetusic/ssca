MELBOURENE GAM

Seven Seas Cruising Association is the largest organization of voyaging cruisers in the world. All of us share the dream of cruising the seas as a lifestyle. Founded in 1952, SSCA is also one of the oldest organizations of cruisers in the world. The goals of the original six founders are still the goals of SSCA today: sharing cruising information, camaraderie and leaving a clean wake. Whether you're a dreamer, a weekend cruiser, an offshore passage maker or somewhere in between, SSCA offers key benefits to enhance your cruising life.
